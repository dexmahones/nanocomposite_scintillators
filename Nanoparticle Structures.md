# Nanoplatelets (NPL)
## Core-shell
>[!quote] ...continuous growth...over the entire surface of...NPLs at high temperatures, can lead to an increase in photoluminescence quantum yield (PLQY) and optical stability by passivating surface defects [[Literature Notes#Expanding the Family of Heterostructured CdSe/CdS Core/Crown Nanoplatelets Six- and Seven-Monolayers-Thick Species| ref]].
## Core-crown
>[!quote]  Crown growth...focuses on the passivation of defects at the edges of the NPLs, whereby the thickness of the core/crown heterostructure remains the same as for the core NPLs and only leads to an increase in the lateral  dimensions [[Literature Notes#Expanding the Family of Heterostructured CdSe/CdS Core/Crown Nanoplatelets Six- and Seven-Monolayers-Thick Species| ref]].

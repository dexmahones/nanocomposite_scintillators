#logbook #fabrication
# Description
These samples were made using art epoxy resin and CdS quantum dots (QDs) provided by UniZulu. The `RT` indicates they were synthesized at room temperature. It is important to note that the QDs had fully dried out by the time they were used, and were not well shielded from external light sources.
# Constituents
- 1.5 ml of two-part epoxy resin mixed in a ratio of A:B -> 10:6
- 16.6 mg of CdS RT quantum dots re-suspended in 4 ml of toluene
- Four different QD concentrations were trialed by varying the ratio of resin to toluene (CdS).
- Two sample pixels (A and B) were made for each QD concentration.
